12 | Page 10. Model Name: Wind-Powered Electricity Generator Working Principle: The model converts wind energy into electrical energy using a small wind turbine and generator. Materials Required: Cardboard, wooden dowels, small generator/motor, PVC pipe, and strong tape. DIY Making Procedure: a. Create blades from cardboard and attach them to the wooden dowels to form a rotor. b. Mount the rotor on a PVC pipe stand and connect it to the small generator/motor. c. Place the wind turbine in front of a fan or in a windy area to observe the generator producing electricity when the blades rotate. Why Is The Model Useful: This model highlights the potential of wind energy as a renewable and clean source of electricity. Tips for Presentation: Explain how wind energy can be harnessed on a larger scale through wind farms and its role in reducing greenhouse gas emissions.

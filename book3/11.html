11 | Page 9. Model Name: Mini Wind Turbine with LED Display Working Principle: The model uses Arduino, a small wind turbine, and LEDs to visualize wind energy generation. Materials Required: Arduino Uno, small wind turbine (e.g., mini DC generator), LEDs, and a breadboard. DIY Making Procedure: a. Connect the wind turbine and LEDs to the Arduino Uno. b. Program the Arduino to read the wind turbine's voltage output and control the LEDs accordingly. c. Observe the LED display lighting up as the wind turbine generates electricity. Why Is The Model Useful: This model demonstrates the concept of wind energy generation and its potential to power LED displays. Tips for Presentation: Discuss the advantages of wind energy and its role in renewable energy sources.

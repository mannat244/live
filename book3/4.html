4 | Page 2. Model Name: Traffic Light Controller Working Principle: The model uses Arduino and LEDs to simulate a traffic light system with automatic control. Materials Required: Arduino Uno, LEDs (Red, Yellow, Green), breadboard, and jumper wires. DIY Making Procedure: a. Connect the LEDs to the Arduino Uno, representing the red, yellow, and green lights of a traffic signal. b. Program the Arduino to control the LEDs in a sequence to simulate traffic light behavior. c. Use a button or motion sensor to trigger the traffic light sequence for pedestrian crossing. Why Is The Model Useful: This model teaches students about traffic control systems and how technology can improve traffic flow and safety. Tips for Presentation: Discuss the importance of traffic management and how smart traffic light systems can reduce congestion and accidents.

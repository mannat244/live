8 | Page 6. Model Name: Eco-Friendly Wind Chime Working Principle: The model uses recycled materials to create a wind chime that produces soothing sounds when the wind blows. Materials Required: Bottle caps, metal spoon, string, beads, and a stick or branch. DIY Making Procedure: a. String together the bottle caps, beads, and metal spoon on separate strings. b. Attach the strings to the stick or branch. c. Hang the wind chime in a windy area and listen to the gentle chimes. Why Is The Model Useful: This model encourages the use of recycled materials to create art and functional items, promoting waste reduction and creativity. Tips for Presentation: Explain the importance of recycling and upcycling in reducing environmental impact.

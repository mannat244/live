11 | Page Working Principle: The model demonstrates the conversion of wind energy into rotational motion using a small wind turbine. Materials Required: Cardboard, wooden dowels, paper cups, plastic propeller, and tape. DIY Making Procedure: a. Cut cardboard pieces to form the base of the wind turbine. b. Attach wooden dowels to create the tower. c. Use paper cups as the blades and attach a plastic propeller to the top. d. Position the wind turbine in front of a fan or blow air towards it to observe the rotation. Why Is The Model Useful: This model introduces the concept of wind energy and its application in generating electricity. Tips for Presentation: Discuss the potential of wind energy in powering small devices and remote locations.
